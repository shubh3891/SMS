OUR EVENTS

  • Our Annual Function • 2021

    Welcome to our spectacular annual function, a celebration of talent, creativity, and unity! We are thrilled to present an enchanting evening filled with unforgettable performances and heartwarming moments that showcase the brilliance of our students.

    This year's theme, "Harmony in Diversity," embodies the essence of our school community, where students from diverse backgrounds come together to create a harmonious symphony of talents. Each act and presentation will reflect the beauty of our differences, emphasizing the strength that lies in our unity.

  • Educational Tour • 2022

    Welcome to our exhilarating educational tour, a journey of discovery, exploration, and boundless learning! We are delighted to embark on this exciting adventure with our students, teachers, and esteemed chaperones.


    This year's educational tour is designed to be an immersive experience, blending fun and education to create lasting memories and invaluable knowledge. As we venture into new landscapes and cultures, we aim to broaden our students' horizons, fostering a sense of curiosity and global awareness.


    TWe extend our deepest gratitude to the parents and guardians whose unwavering trust and support have made this educational tour possible. Your belief in the power of experiential learning will undoubtedly enrich the lives of our students and leave an indelible mark on their educational journey.

    It is with overwhelming joy and a profound sense of accomplishment that I stand before you today to share a proud moment in my life. I am deeply honored and humbled to receive the prestigious award for "Top Student of the Year."


    This recognition is not just a personal triumph but a testament to the unwavering support of my teachers, mentors, and my family, who have believed in me every step of the way. Their guidance and encouragement have been the guiding light that led me to this remarkable achievement.
